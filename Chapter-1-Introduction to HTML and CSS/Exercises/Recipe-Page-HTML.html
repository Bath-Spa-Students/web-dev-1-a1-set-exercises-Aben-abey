Homemade Noodle Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center of the flour mixture.
  2. Gradually add the warm water to the flour mixture, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon or spatula until a soft dough forms. If the dough seems too dry, add a little more water. If it seems too wet, add a little more flour.
  3. Knead the dough for a few minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. If it's too sticky, add a little more flour. If it's too dry, add a little more water.
  4. Lightly flour a clean surface and roll out the dough to a thickness of about 1/2 inch. Cut the dough into strips about 12 inches long.
  5. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Cook the noodles in batches, turning them occasionally, until they are golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
  6. To serve, place the cooked noodles in a large bowl. Drizzle with honey, if desired. Toss to combine and serve hot.
