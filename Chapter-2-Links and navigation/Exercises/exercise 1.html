Plot

In a dystopian realm of Panem, Katniss Everdeen, a symbol of resistance, volunteers for the Hunger Games to save her sister. Amidst brutal survival challenges, she challenges the Capitol's tyranny, igniting a rebellion. Her resilience echoes through the districts, inspiring hope and defiance against oppressive forces.

Cast

  • Josh Hutcherson as Peeta Mellark
  • Liam Hemsworth as Gale Hawthorne

Katniss Everdeen

Katniss Everdeen is a resourceful and skilled archer from District 12. Her bravery and determination make her a symbol of hope for the oppressed citizens of Panem.

Peeta Mellark

Peeta Mellark is a compassionate baker's son from District 12 who harbors genuine feelings for Katniss. He demonstrates strength and resilience throughout the Hunger Games.

Gale Hawthorne

Gale Hawthorne is Katniss's close friend from District 12 and a skilled hunter. He supports Katniss and her family, but his feelings for her become complicated as the series progresses.
